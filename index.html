Explore Sapao

Nestled along the serene coastline of Guiuan, Eastern Samar, Barangay Sapao offers the perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Known for its peaceful community and rich coastal resources, Sapao is a destination worth discovering—whether you're a traveler seeking an off-the-beaten-path adventure or someone looking to reconnect with the simplicity of provincial life.
