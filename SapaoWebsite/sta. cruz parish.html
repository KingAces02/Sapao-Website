Santa Cruz Parish Sapao

Santa Cruz Parish in Barangay Sapao is more than just a place of worship—it is the spiritual heart of the community and a symbol of faith, unity, and resilience for the people of Sapao. Officially established as a quasi-parish on March 5, 1999, and later canonically erected as a full parish on September 14, 2004, Santa Cruz Parish operates under the Diocese of Borongan. Despite being a relatively young parish compared to centuries-old churches in the region, it has quickly become a strong religious and cultural institution within the barangay. The church is dedicated to the Holy Cross (Santa Cruz), and its annual fiesta, celebrated on May 7, is one of the most anticipated events in the community. During this time, Sapao comes alive with vibrant religious processions, novena prayers, music, and food-sharing traditions that bring together not only residents but also families who return home from other provinces and cities just to celebrate.

Santa Cruz Parish is also known for its deep commitment to youth and community engagement. Its Parish Youth Ministry is very active throughout the year, especially during the liturgical seasons of Advent and Lent, when spiritual recollections, choir workshops, and charity programs are held. The parish fosters a strong sense of belonging among young people, encouraging them to grow in faith, leadership, and service.

Regular Masses are held at 5:45 AM from Tuesday to Sunday, giving parishioners a consistent opportunity for daily devotion and reflection, while Monday is reserved as a rest day. The church building itself may be modest, but its strength lies in the people who fill it—with their prayers, their voices, and their shared commitment to keeping faith alive. In a fast-changing world, Santa Cruz Parish serves as a steady anchor for Sapao, preserving Catholic traditions while nurturing a dynamic and compassionate community. It stands not only as a place of religious observance but also as a space where values, identity, and hope are continually renewed.
